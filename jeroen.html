Michael "Imaqtpie" Santana

Ex League of Legends Pro / Livestreamer

Michael Santana, better known as Imaqtpie, is an American League of Legends player. Formerly a professional player for Team Dignitas since 2011, he retired from professional play in 2014 to pursue a full-time streaming career on Twitch.tv. He is one of the largest League of Legends online personalities with over two million followers on Twitch.tv. He reportedly earns over $2 million annually streaming on Twitch.
